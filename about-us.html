What is Pag-IBIG?

Pag-IBIG is an acronym which means for Pagtutulungan sa Kinabukasan: Ikaw, Bangko, Industriya at Gobyerno.

Pag-IBIG is a government-owned and managed organization that administers the national savings program and provides affordable housing financing to Filipinos.

Mission and Vission

The Vision of PAG-IBIG Fund is for every Filipino work to save with Pag-IBIG Fund and to have decent shelter.

The Mission of PAG-IBIG Fund is to generate more savings from more Filipino workers, to administer a sustinable Fund with integrity, sound financial principles, and with social responsibility, and to provide accessible funds for housing of every member.

Values and Objectives

The governing values that will steer the Pag-IBIG Fund in pursuit of it's Vision to include Professionalism, Integirty, Excellence, and Service.

The objectives of the Pag-IBIG Fund is to sustian the membership growth and retention that would results to a P2 Billion annual increase in members' savings collection until 2028, and provide affordable home financing to at least 691,899 low-income earners through socialized and low-cost housing from 2023 until 2028.
